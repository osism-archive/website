Deployment and lifecycle management framework for OpenStack and Ceph

The Open Source Infrastructure & Service Manager is a powerful deployment and lifecycle management framework for OpenStack and Ceph as well as required services such as a RabbitMQ broker or a MariaDB Galera cluster.

OpenStack provides a proven and reliable foundation for platforms such as Gitlab CI, Kubernetes, Cloud Foundry, OpenShift, or High Performance Computing (HPC).

OSISM has been successfully deployed on a variety of production environments for over 4 years for operations and lifecycle management. Contact us for more information on our success stories.

Sovereign Cloud Stack

OSISM is an integral part of the Sovereign Cloud Stack (SCS) project funded by the Spring Innovation Agency. It will thus play a major role in the GAIA-X project.

Enterprise Edition

OSISM is a 100% open source solution, built on open source building blocks, and it is developed openly and transparently. For use in enterprise productions, we recommend the use of the OSISM Enterprise Edition. This enables the use of LTS versions, managed services, professional support, vulnerability scanning, signed images, and more.

The Enterprise Edition will be fully SCS compliant and certified, as soon as a relevant certification is available. This enables the baseline for building future GAIA-X compliant cloud solutions.

Contact us for more information about the Enterprise Edition.
